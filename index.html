About Me

I'm Carlos Chavez — a media communications specialist and creative technologist blending design, branding, and storytelling across digital platforms. With a passion for audio-visual work and front-end development, I’ve supported brands, events, and tech launches with a focus on bold visual identity and clean execution.

From developing juice brand websites to managing social media for music tours and launching futuristic tech visuals, I love crafting experiences that connect with people. Whether I’m editing podcasts, designing for a drop, or building a microsite — I show up with curiosity, style, and grit.

Maverick City Worship

I worked alongside the media team for Maverick City Tour, capturing backstage moments, live performances, and crowd energy. I helped edit and publish dynamic short-form video content, tour recap reels, and coordinated daily updates across social platforms for audience retention and growth.
